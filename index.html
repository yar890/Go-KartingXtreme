Go-Karting Xtreme

Go-Karting Xtreme is the biggest community run Go-Karting game on ROBLOX. We aim to provide the best Go-Karting racing experience compared to all other existing Go-Karting games.

What makes us unique?

  • Jobs with a purpose
  • Amazing Atmosphere
  • Time Tabled Session

We are the only Go-karting Group that has Executives trained for a certain job in the business E.G. Marketing.

We create a great atmosphere within the race track with kind staff and a designed briefing room. We care about our players, so we aim to make all players welcomed to our game.

You know when the sessions are because of are time-table, no need to look at the shout all the time.

Reception

At Go-Karting Xtreme, we have a unique reception system as part of our session system. Here you can book 30 min or 1-hour sessions, depending on what sessions are available.

Control Room

Our control room allows 3 major changes to occur on the track. The spawn kart system spawns in new karts. The light system controls the kart speed and changes the lights on the track. The pit gate system opens or closes the pit area.
