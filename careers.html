Operation Department

Operation Department

The host department is incharge of sessions. They are the essential members of staff that typically work on the track.

Track Staff

Track Staff operate in 3 areas at Go-Karting Xtreme

Safety Crew

The role of a safety crew is to deal with issues on the track and provide a safety briefing before each session.

Receptionist

The role of a Receptionist is to book sessions and answer questions for racers at Go-Karting Xtreme.

Control Room Crew

The role of a Control Room Crew is to control the lights, gates and karts. You will need to listen to Safety crew and the host.

Apply Now!

Supervisor

Supervisor assist the Racing host

The role of a supervisor is to assist the racing host. You will be responsible for monitoring and helping staff in a specific area of Track Staff assigned by the Racing Host. This role is earned through promotion only.

Promotion Only

Racing Host

Racing host is the role were you are in charge of running individual session.

The role of a racing host is to lead sessions. You will be responsible for running individual sessions and ensuring that the sessions run smoothly. You will be trained to do all track staff roles, including host training.

Note: This role has high demand. Expect delays.

Career FAQ

When will my application be processed?

It can take up to 2 weeks for application forms to be fully processed. 

When does training and interviews start?

We host training and interviews on a weekly basis. Check the Discord for training and interview announcements.

Why did I fail my application form?

For information on why you failed your application form, go to the #application_feedback channel on our Discord.

Note: You fail your application form instantly if we can't find you in our Discord.

Why do I need Discord?

Discord is essential for the operation of Go-Karting Xtreme. It is used for effective communication between staff members.
